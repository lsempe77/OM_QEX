# FIELD DEFINITIONS

## Outcome Fields - EXTRACT EACH OM-IDENTIFIED OUTCOME

You MUST extract ALL outcomes listed in the OM GUIDANCE section. For each one:

- **outcome_name**: Short name (use OM's outcome_category as guidance)
- **outcome_description**: Detailed description including units, measurement method
- **effect_size**: The regression coefficient, standardized mean difference, or treatment effect
- **p_value**: Exact p-value (extract the number, e.g., 0.023, not "p < 0.05")
- **standard_error**: Standard error of the estimate (often in parentheses after coefficient)
- **confidence_interval_lower**: Lower bound of 95% CI (if reported)
- **confidence_interval_upper**: Upper bound of 95% CI (if reported)
- **literal_text**: Exact verbatim text showing this outcome's estimate
  - Example: "Project | 4,408*** (1,369)" or "Asset wealth: 0.31 (0.09), p=0.001"
- **text_position**: Location in paper
  - Example: "Table 6, Row 'Project', Column '(3) Amount savings'"

## Study Design Fields

- **evaluation_design**: Type of study
  - "Randomized Controlled Trial", "Difference-in-Differences", "Regression Discontinuity", etc.

## Statistical Fields

- **sample_size_treatment**: Number of participants in treatment group
- **sample_size_control**: Number of participants in control group

## Graduation Program Components

For EACH component, classify as "Yes", "No", or "Not mentioned":
- **consumption_support**: Cash or in-kind transfers
- **healthcare**: Health services or insurance
- **assets**: Livestock, tools, productive assets
- **skills_training**: Training for asset management
- **savings**: Savings accounts or microfinance
- **coaching**: Mentoring or motivational support
- **social_empowerment**: Community mobilization, women's empowerment activities

# EXTRACTION RULES

1. **Use OM Guidance**: The outcomes listed below were already identified - find them in the paper using the location hints
2. **Extract ALL OM outcomes**: Every outcome in the OM GUIDANCE section should appear in your outcomes array
3. **Extract exact values**: For effect_size and p_value, extract the numerical values exactly as reported
4. **Standard errors**: Often shown in parentheses after coefficients, e.g., "4,408 (1,369)" means effect=4408, SE=1369
5. **Significance stars**: *** usually means p<0.01, ** means p<0.05, * means p<0.10
   - If exact p-value not given but stars shown, estimate: *** → 0.01, ** → 0.05, * → 0.10
6. **Missing data**: Use null for any field that cannot be found
7. **Units matter**: Include units in outcome_description (e.g., "Malawian Kwacha", "USD", "index 0-1")
